About Bunyore Bee Fiber

Connecting Bunyore to the world with cutting-edge fiber optic technology

Your Trusted Local Internet Service Provider

Bunyore Bee Fiber Network is a locally owned and operated internet service provider dedicated to bringing high-speed fiber optic internet to homes and businesses throughout Bunyore and surrounding areas.

Founded in 2020, we've grown from a small startup to the region's leading fiber provider, thanks to our commitment to reliability, customer service, and community development.

Up to 1Gbps symmetrical connections for seamless browsing, streaming, and gaming.
